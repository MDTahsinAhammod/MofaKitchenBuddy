    Recipe Title: Rice Pudding
    Ingredients: * 1 cup cooked rice
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups milk
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
* Ground cinnamon or nutmeg for topping (optional)
    Instructions: 1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
2. Add the cooked rice to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
3. Pour the mixture into a greased 8-inch baking dish.
4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 45-55 minutes, or until the pudding is set and slightly golden on top.
5. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon or nutmeg, if desired, before serving.

    Recipe Title: Sweet Noodle Pudding
    Ingredients: * 1 cup cooked rice
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 egg
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)
    Instructions: 1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the rice, milk, egg, sugar, and vanilla extract.
3. Stir in the chopped nuts, if desired.
4. Pour the mixture into a greased 8x8 inch baking dish.
5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the pudding is set and the top is golden brown.
6. Serve warm or cold.

**Recipe Title:** Classic Egg Noodle Kugel

**Ingredients:**

* 1 pound wide egg noodles
* 1/2 cup vegetable oil
* 1 large onion, finely chopped
* 6 large eggs, lightly beaten
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
* 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
* 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 2 cups milk
* 1/2 cup sour cream or crème fraîche (optional)
* 1/4 cup bread crumbs (optional, for topping)
* 2 tablespoons melted butter (optional, for topping)

**Instructions:**

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
2. Cook egg noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain well and set aside.
3. In a large skillet, sauté the onion in vegetable oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
4. In a large bowl, whisk together the beaten eggs, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
5. Add the cooked noodles, sautéed onion, milk, and sour cream (if using) to the egg mixture. Stir until well combined.
6. Pour the noodle mixture into the prepared baking dish.
7. If using, sprinkle the bread crumbs evenly over the top. Drizzle with melted butter.
8. Bake for 45-60 minutes, or until the kugel is golden brown and set. A knife inserted into the center should come out clean.
9. Let cool slightly before serving.  Serve warm or at room temperature.


**Note:**  This is a basic recipe. You can adjust the spices to your liking and add other ingredients, such as raisins, apples, or cheese.  Some recipes also include potatoes or other starches for a richer, denser kugel.
